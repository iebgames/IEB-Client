### _**HOW TO USE**_
         - Just Press **Tab** To Open The Menu And You Can Change The Shortcut From The Minecraft Settings!
         - **Right Click** A Hack To Add Shortcuts To Them
         - **Middle Click** A Hack To See/Edit Its Settings
         - **Middle Click** On A Number To Manually Type Its Value
         - **Ctrl + Mouse Wheel** Inside GUI To Zoom In/Out
         - **Drag Panel Header** To Reposition Any Category
